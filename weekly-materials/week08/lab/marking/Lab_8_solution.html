Task 5 - Evidence#

Is there evidence that the difference in males versus females promoted was not due to chance?

The answer is not graded but aim to share your answer with the TA during the lab.

2.76% of the simulations were more extreme than the observed value of the difference assuming that the labels M, F were randomly shuffled. It‚Äôs unlikely that the difference is due to random chance. This means that this data supports that males were promoted more often than females.

The p-value of the test is below.

pvalue = (
    (sim_results >= observed_diff).sum() 
    + (sim_results < -1 * observed_diff).sum()
    ) / 5000

pvalue

0.0276
